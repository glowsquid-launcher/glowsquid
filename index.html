Glowsquid


Hello!

Glowsquid is a Minecraft launcher which focuses primarly on support for the Fabric modloader.
The launcher is still in development, although you can look for updates on our Discord.

Features

✅ - Instances (have a separate folder, etc. for each version of Minecraft)

✅ - Fabric mod support

✅ - Downloading mods from Modrinth

⚠️ - Downloading mods from other platforms using a special format (in progress)

❌ - Forge support (coming in the future)

❌ - Global datapacks and settings (coming in the future)
